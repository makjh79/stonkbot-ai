## Thesis-exit churn fix
- The bot bought COIN/APP at 16:02 UTC and sold them at 16:07 UTC on a "MACD histogram turns negative for 2+ days" thesis exit, then re-bought APP. This was churn.
- Root cause: `trading_bot.py::ThesisManager.check_thesis_exits()` checked `if macd_hist < 0:` and exited immediately, ignoring the intended 2-day confirmation.
- Patched `/opt/stonk-ai/trading_bot.py`:
  - Added 24-hour minimum hold for all thesis exits to prevent same-hour flip-flops.
  - Added `macd_hist_negative_days` counter; exit fires only after 2 consecutive negative days.
  - New thesis records initialize `macd_hist_negative_days: 0`.
- Restarted `stonk-ai.service` at 2026-07-01 16:17 UTC.
