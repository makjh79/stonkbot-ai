Get Accurate Premarket Data
============================

1. Sign up free at: https://finnhub.io
2. Get your API key from dashboard
3. Add to environment:
   export FINNHUB_API_KEY="your_key_here"

Finnhub provides:
- Real-time premarket quotes (4AM-9:30AM ET)
- Extended hours included in free tier
- 60 API calls/minute
- US stocks only

This will give you the same accurate data as your Futubull screenshot.
