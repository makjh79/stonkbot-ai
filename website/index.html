🤖 How does the AI work? ▼
Autonomous Trading System:

🛡️ Protect: Hard stops at -15% on all positions
🎯 Profit Taking: Trim 25% at +25%, full exit at +50%
🚀 Capital Recycling: Trim 25% when RSI > 75 (euphoria) to fund new entries
🔥 Auto-Entry: Buys when RSI ≤ 35 AND AI Score ≥ 60 with 1.5x volume

3-Tier Watchlist System: Dynamic 15-stock list updates every 5 minutes
🔥 NOW: RSI ≤ 35 + AI ≥ 75 — Highest conviction, bot auto-buys
👀 WATCH: RSI ≤ 48 + AI ≥ 60 — Bot buys if RSI drops below 35
📋 MONITOR: All others — Waiting for setup

Watchlist Entry Criteria:
• RSI 28-48 (buy zone)
• AI Score ≥ 60 (quality)
• Positive momentum vs S&P 500

Auto-Removal Rules:
• RSI > 50 — moved past buy zone
• Up > 5% daily — entry missed
• 3 days without RSI < 40 — stale
• Lagging S&P by 3% — weak setup

Executes during market hours only. No emotions, no exceptions.
